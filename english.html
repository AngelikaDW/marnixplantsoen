1e Marnixplantsoen: a great place

1e Marnixplantsoen is a great place in the city center of Amsterdam. Lots of people are using the facilites, such as:

  • the coolest slide of Amsterdam
  • the skater pool
  • the basketball court
  • the drinking fountains
  • the playground

But it could be much better:

The grassfield, the biggest part of the square, seems only to be for dogs.

Due to staying dogs and dog poo the grass field cannot be used to play or to have a picknick.

Can it be organized better?

At the Fredrick Hendricks Pantsoen nearby you can see how a smart use of the area generates space for all to enjoy the greenery. There is space to play, to walk through the grass fields or sit down. Indeed, there is space for dogs to run - clearly spearated from the other areas.

Our proposal for the Municipality of Amsterdam

Together we will provide a proposal how to better organize the 1e Marnixplantsoen. Join us and we will approach the Municipality Amsterdam and propose to work on a detailed plan together.

1e Marnixplantsoen is ready for a upgrade

Our proposal:

  • a clear separation of "no dogs"(red) and "dogs allowed"(yellow) area
  • low fences and bushes around the part and to separate the areas
  • construction of a walking path through the grass field
  • betere illumination at night by placing mire lampposts
  • a refurbishment of the lawn, filling the holes in the grass
  • installation of wooden benches

Get involved! Join us and support our proposal

Via this website we will keep you informed and updated. In June 2016 we will engage with the municipality of Amsterdam.
